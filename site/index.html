lottery

a guy prays to God:
Dear Lord, please make me win the lottery.
The next day he begs the Lord again:
Please make it so I win the lottery, Lord!
The next day, He again prays:
Please, please, dear Lord, make me win the lottery!
Suddenly he hears a voice from above:
would you kindly go and buy a lottery ticket.

broken car

Police officer talks to a driver:
Your tail light is broken, your tires must be exchanged and your bumper hangs halfway down.
That will be 300 dollars.
Driver: Alright, go ahead. They want twice as much as that at the garage.
